Worse For Wear :,( Fall '16 campaign. A ready to wear collection of LQQKs designed with a patterned custom fabric designed using circular repetitive scribbles (sourced from the artist’s old notebooks and school essays) as an unintended by-product of the artist’s struggle with depression and OCD as a child. Shot by Elizabeth Renstrom. Worn by Rafia. Designed by Sessa Englund’s teenage angst.
